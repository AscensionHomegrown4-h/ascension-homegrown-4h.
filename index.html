Ascension Homegrown 4-H

Who We Are

A community 4-H club in Ascension Parish for HOMESCHOOLERS and MEMBERS-AT-LARGE, welcoming new members ages 5–18.

Meetings

Held on the second Thursday of every month. Next meeting: October 12.

Cloverbuds and Youth

Cloverbuds are K–3rd grade. Youth are 4th–12th grade.

Opportunities

Participate in Travel, Agriculture, Service, Citizenship, Outdoors, Healthy Living, Teamwork, Science and Technology, Friendship, and so much more! Join project clubs, receive scholarships, and go to camps around the state.
